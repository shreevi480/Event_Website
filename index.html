About the Event

Join us for the grand House Cup Award Ceremony, where the winning house will be announced amidst a magical evening filled with floating candles, a grand feast, and colorful house banners. Witness the excitement and pride as students eagerly await the results of the year's house points competition.

  • 6:00 PM - Opening Ceremony
  • 7:00 PM - House Points Announcement
  • 8:00 PM - Feast and Celebration
  • 10:00 PM - End of Ceremony
